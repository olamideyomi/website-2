About Us

We are the world's largest university

City Tech is a dynamic and diverse public liberal arts college that fosters academic excellence through close interaction among students, faculty and staff. City Tech weaves the values of integrity, community engagement, and global citizenship into the fabric of its academic programs and campus life. In an environment that cultivates critical thinking, empathy, creativity and intercultural understanding, we endeavor to stimulate a passion for learning and a commitment to building a more just and sustainable world. The College is a community of students, teachers, staff, and alumni bound together in mutual support, respect, and dedication to the Mission.
